1. A Couple's ABV (Alcohol by Volume) Adventure: Harmonizing Contrasts


Meet Marija and Bob, a couple who share a love of travel and a passion for beer. However, their beer preferences couldn't be more different. Marija, drawn to bold, intense flavors, dreams of exploring the world with ABV Adventure: The Bold Bataillon. As for Bob, who has a taste for subtlety and finesse, envisions a journey characterized by Adventure: The Soft Symphony.

1.1. ABV Adventure: The Soft Symphony

Welcome to The Soft Symphony, an enchanting journey through countries known for their nuanced beers with lower alcohol percentages. Imagine walking through the bustling streets of Trinidad and Tobago, where the gentle melodies of local beers dance on your palate. Bosnia-Herzegovina, with its rich cultural tapestry, offers a serenade of subtle flavours, while Albania's beers are a symphony of finesse. Exploring the softer side of beer in destinations like Jersey and Lebanon immerses you in a world where each sip becomes a delicate note in a harmonious composition.

The Soft Symphony is not just a tour, it's a sensory experience, an exploration of countries that have mastered the art of brewing with a soft touch.



Top locations ranked by beer strength (softest beers)


This ranking is quite original! If you can't handle drinking a lot, but still like to taste a lot of different flavours, these regions are perfect for you. In fact, we've made sure to keep places with a good variety of beers, at least 5 beers per country, and which have been rated at least 20 times in total on both sites. You'll be able to drink, drink, drink without ever getting a headache!




1.2. ABV Adventure: The Bold Battalion


Walk alongside The Bold Battalion, an adventurous tour designed for those with the most audacious palates, individuals seeking bold and robust beer experiences. Imagine yourself immersed in the heart of Belgium, surrounded by the rich history and distinctive allure of Trappist beers alongside a selection of robust, flavorful ales. Estonia, the hidden gem of the beer world, offers a battalion of craft beers which won’t lack punch. Egypt, with its ancient brewing traditions, unleashes a specter of flavors that will captivate your taste buds.

Crossing the battlefields of Iceland and the Netherlands, every sip becomes a bold statement. Bold Battalion isn't just a tour, it's a conquest of countries where beer is a force to be reckoned with. From Rhode Island's bold IPAs to the Netherlands, each destination is a bastion of the strongest, creating a symphony of intensity for bold palates.

Here, it's the other way around: you have to be brave to embark on this journey. The beers you'll be drinking are monstrous! Fasten your seatbelts, and take a good bottle of water with you, as it might come in handy if you overindulge. However, we remind you that alcohol abuse is dangerous for your health, so stay reasonable! It's worth noting that the destinations for this trip are very varied around the world, which tells you that people all over the world appreciate strong beers!
