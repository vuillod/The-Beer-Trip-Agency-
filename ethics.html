BTA ethics


Our approach to designing beer-themed trips is grounded in ethical considerations that prioritize responsible consumption, cultural appreciation, and the promotion of diverse experiences. As a trip agency, we utilize a data set derived from beer reviews and ratings to craft unique itineraries that cater to varying preferences within the beer community. Our commitment to ethics involves promoting trips that celebrate the artistry of brewing, cultural diversity, and responsible alcohol consumption. We strive to highlight the craftsmanship of local breweries, fostering a sense of community while encouraging moderation. Additionally, our thematic trips are designed to educate participants about the rich history and cultural significance of beer, fostering a deep appreciation for the craft. We are dedicated to ensuring that our beer-themed trips not only provide enjoyable experiences but also contribute positively to the communities visited, supporting local economies and sustainable practices.

Furthermore, our trips will be organised in such a way as to reduce our carbon footprint as much as possible.
